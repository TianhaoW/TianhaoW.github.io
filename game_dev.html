Plans for GamePlay and Plot:

The heroine's name will be Lunaria which originates from the word "Luna" and "Lunatic", and this will be related to the plot.

Lunaria has the ability to use both sword and magic, and the magic is further classified into 6 categories: Light, Dark, Fire, Ice, Life, Death. There is no integrated levels in the game, yet each magic category and the sword has their own independent levels. Lunaria will gain new abilities or spells by leveling up in the specific category. Some spells also require certain levels in more than one magic categories.

Future Updates:
  • Add more spells for Lunaria (this will be added to the Demo)
  • Modify the jump function with acceleration (instead of constant speed for jump and fall)
